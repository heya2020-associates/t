Sushi - Japan

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, usually accompanied by seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It is typically served as bite-sized pieces and is often eaten with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

History: Sushi originated in Japan around the 8th century as a way to preserve fish in fermented rice. Over time, it evolved into the dish we know today.
